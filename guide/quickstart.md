# 📘 LifeOS Quickstart

Everything you need to run your day. One page. Pin it.

---

## The daily rhythm

| When | What arrives | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **7:45 AM** | **Morning dashboard** — email + Telegram digest + voice note | Skim the **Top 10 actions**. Each one with a draft has the full text in an expandable block — tap, copy, paste, send. |
| **All day** | **Reminders** ping at the right time; **Grace** may ask a question | Act on reminders. Reply to Grace's questions in-thread — she files your answer automatically. |
| **8:00 PM** | **Evening brief** — inbox triage, reply drafts in Gmail, tomorrow's prep, people to reconnect with | Sweep your Gmail Drafts folder (replies are pre-written). Confirm any calendar suggestions. |

That's it. ~10 minutes of decisions across the day.

---

## Talking to Grace (your Telegram bot)

Just text her naturally. She handles:

| Say something like… | She does |
|---|---|
| `remind me at 4pm to call the lawyer` | Schedules a phone reminder (natural phrasing — no format needed) |
| `remind me tomorrow 9am to review the deck` | Same, for tomorrow |
| `capture: idea for a new product line` | Files it into your knowledge vault |
| `skip gym today` | Removes today's gym block (without penalizing the habit) |
| `discrete 3h` | Voice briefs go business-only for 3 hours (for when others are around) |
| `status` | Quick system health check |
| *(answering a question she asked)* | Reply in-thread — it's written straight into the right system |

If she can't parse something, she says "noted — next run will handle it" and a smarter pass picks it up. Nothing is lost.

## The weekly rhythm

| Day | Event | Your part |
|---|---|---|
| **Sunday** | **Network Weave** — proposed intros, gap-bridges, idea→action items | Approve/veto the intros (all double-opt-in). |
| **Monday** | **Weekly Debrief** — ~10 quick questions | Answer them (~5 min). This is the **only** scheduled thing that needs you. |
| **Friday** | **CEO Report** — one picture of the whole operation | Read it. Notice trends. |

---

## On-demand commands (type these when you want them)

- `unsubscribe-audit` — clean out a spammy inbox
- `meeting-prep [name]` — full brief before a call
- `wellness` — your habit/time-block scoreboard
- `telegram-scan` — pull alpha/news from your channels
- `social-checkin` — refresh contacts from LinkedIn/Facebook

---

## If something looks off

- **No morning dashboard?** Your Mac was asleep. It catches up on next wake, or run it manually.
- **A draft is wrong?** Just edit it before sending — the system learns from your edits.
- **Too many / too few reminders?** Tell Grace; the buffers are tunable.
