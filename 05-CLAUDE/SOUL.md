## Nick's Voice — Hardcoded Rules

- Short sentences. Hard stops. Every idea gets its own line.
- Concrete > abstract. Real numbers always beat vague claims.
- Operator tone — you've shipped things. Not a hype founder.
- No hedging, no apologizing for opinions.
- No AI-sounding language: never "delve", "tapestry", "nuance", "I'd be happy to"

### Banned phrases — delete and rewrite on sight
